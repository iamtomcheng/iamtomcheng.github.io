Particle Physics Reference Links

  • Spin Correlations of Lambda anti-Lambda Pairs as a Probe of Quark-Antiquark Pair Production A paper from John Ellis suggesting the possibility of measuring Lambda-Lambda bar spin correlation as a probe of quark-antiquark pair production mechanism.
  • Parton Fragmentation and String Dynamics This is the most comprehensive reference of the Lund string fragmentation model.
  • Recent Developments in the Lund Model A brief introduction to the famous Lund string fragmentation model and its consequences.
  • Spin Formalism and Applications to New Physics Searches A very nice reference on spin techniques used in particle physics.
  • Spin in Particle Physics A thorough introduction to spin and its role in elementary particle physics.
  • Spin, Mass, and Symmetry An introduction to the theory of spin in quantum field theory presented by Michael E. Peskin.
  • Introduction to QCD A pedagogical note on QCD at graduate level written by Prof. Peter Skands, author of PYTHIA generator.
  • Notes from Sidney Coleman's Physics 253a Notes taken by Brian Hill during Sidney Coleman's lectures on Quantum Field Theory (Physics 253), given at Harvard University in Fall semester of the 1986-1987 academic year.
